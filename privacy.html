Last updated: 1st August 2023

We may also collect non-personal information, such as:

We take data security seriously and have implemented appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information held by Bondt. To exercise these rights or for any privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at support@bondt.io.
